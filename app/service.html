Over nearly fifteen years, Green Peak has completed thousands of talent and organi­zational engagements for our demanding private equity and corporate clients. We build value across the deal cycle, offering diagnostic and execution support from diligence through exit.

1. DILIGENCE SUPPORT

  • Evaluate fit of team, culture, and organization against your investment thesis
  • Determine change readiness and scalability of executive team
  • Provide prioritized recommendations and an ‘Organizational Blueprint for Scalability’

2. BUY RIGHT

  • Advise on founder or seller’s financial and career motivations
  • Discover red flags that impact purchase price
  • Client negotiates attractive valuation and deal terms

3. FIRST 100 DAYS

  • Align investor and senior team on critical priorities
  • Comprehensive organizational review identifying health baseline and key improvement targets
  • Establishment of action teams (make plural) to pursue organizational priorities

4. EXECUTION AND VALUE MAXIMIZATION

  • Finalist candidate assessments during talent upgrades
  • Organizational due diligence for acquisitions to platform investment

  • Board optimization: composition and effectiveness
  • Succession planning for current team and go forward team at exit

5. PREPARING FOR HIGH VALUE EXIT

  • Organizational review to position company for maximum valuation
  • Coach executive team on consistent message around value maximization
  • Prepare executive team for diligence interviews around talent and organizational topics

6. SELL HIGH

  • Client negotiates maximum value
